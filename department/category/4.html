This is one of the indispensable departments of SCIENT This is an inter disciplinary department where subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English & Environmental Sciences etc. are included. The department being an integral and basic part of college focuses on promoting the discovery & application of scientific knowledge in a manner that prepares the students to contribute to society effectively, intellectually and ethically as citizens of a dynamic scientific community. The department imparts excellent humanity.

The department caters the needs of B.Tech & M.B.A. with reference to Applied Sciences and Humanities. There is an active role of department in all activities like college administration, Examination, transportation, extra-curricular activities etc.

The department of H&S comprising Mathematics, English, Physics, and Chemistry etc. was established in the year 2001. Faculty with proven teaching and research capabilities from reputed Institutions are available in the department. 
The department tries to keep pace with the rapid advancement in technology by providing well equipped labs and by teaching the students the latest trends in the field. The theory and practicals are taught with the idea of Supporting their engineering subject
Developing a research oriented approach.
Enhancing their understanding about every day sciences.The Department is enriched with fully furnished PC based communication Lab which emphasises on improving the communication skills of the students by organising regular group discussions etc. An interactive spoken Language Lab with modern audio equipment is  available.
 We are maintaining Departmental library along with centralised library, which includes 120 titles.
