The Department of Computer Science and Engineering came into existence with the establishment of the institute in 2001 with the intake of 60 in UG (B. Tech) and later enhanced to 120 .The PG (M. Tech CSE) started with intake of 18 in 2012 and enhanced to 42 . Department aims to provide students with strong conceptual foundations and also expose them to the forefront of development in the field of computing. Our goal is to give an Engineering attitude for confidence building to solve technical problems. The department has highly qualified and experienced faculty members. The department maintains its own departmental library as well.The state-of-art laboratories have over 450 computers with the latest configuration PCs and are completely networked, which provides adequate facilities for curriculum need like DBMS lab, Java Lab, Data structures lab WT labs etc. Emphasis is placed on regular attendance, general discipline, conceptual understanding, logical perception and project work.Department of Computer Science works as the controlling centrer of computing for the entire institute and provide internet connectivity to various departments through its servers and switches. Department also has dedicated labs for internet access, visual classes for effective teaching. The Department has well experienced and highly qualified faculty, all are M.Tech and some doing their Research in various domains.

As a part of the curriculum, Industrial visits are arranged for students of B.Tech ¾ (CSE) in II-Semester. Students of our Department actively participate in National-level Student Paper Presentation Contests being organized at various Engineering Colleges and Universities. A few of them have been awarded in these Paper presentations contests. The department has been organized National-level Technical Paper Presentation under the banner of SAMVIGYAN . This event is conducted in every year. SAMVIGYAN 2K14 conducted on 19th We are maintaining Departmental library along with centralized library ,which includes 182 titles, 206 Volumes, 3 national journals ,10 international journals ,7 magazines, 68 project reports and Subject CDs-275.

We have each lab facility with 90 systems, 4 IBM Servers and all necessary software with licensed versions. We have separate P.G Lab with 24 systems with Wi-Fi connectivity and also have separate project lab exclusively for project development at B.Tech level and M. Tech level.
