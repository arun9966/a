The College is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University,Hyderabad. The student admissions are governed by the rules and regulations of TSCHE.

The student admissions are based on the rank obtained in EAMCET / ICET entrance tests for B.Tech and MBA respectively.

The Student admissions are based on the rank obtained in GATE / PG-ECET entrance tests for M.Tech (any discplain).

Lateral Entry:

20% of students are allotted for students who complete three years diploma to join in the 2nd year B.Tech program. The admissions are governed by the rules and regulations of TSCHE and ECET.

B-Category:

The College has a provision to admit the B-Category students/in lieu of B-Category in each branch (except Diploma) every year. 30% of the sanctioned intake is being filled with the B-Category students, which is in line with the eligibility criteria laid down by the Govt. of Telangana.

Fees : For Convener seat it is Rs.50,200/-

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR ADMISSIONS (CAT B SEATS) AND ORDER OF MERIT:

(i)  NRI - The institutions that are approved by All India Council for Technical Education andpermitted to fill NRI seats not exceeding 15% of the sanctioned intake in each course for theacademic year shall admit NRI candidates (sons and daughters of NRIs) who have passed thequalifying examination with not less than 50% marks in the prescribed group subjects or50% aggregate marks in the qualifying examination or Cumulative Grade Point Average(CGPA) equivalent to 5 on a scale of 10. 
(Non Resident Indian (NRI) candidate means a candidate born to a parent of Indian originresiding outside the country and who has passed the qualifying examination or its equivalent‚Äù.)

(ii)  JEE (Mains) - The remaining seats shall be filled on merit basis with candidates includingfrom other States and Union territories who have given All India rank at JEE (Mains) andsecured not less than 45% of marks in the prescribed group subjects in the qualifying  examination.

(iii) TSEAMCET - The seats remaining unfilled from the above shall be filled with eligiblecandidates, who have qualified the TSEAMCET examination on merit basis. 

(iv)  Thereafter, if any seats still remain unfilled such seats may be filled on merit basis withcandidates securing not less than 45% (40% in case of candidates belonging to reservedcategories) of marks in the prescribed group subjects taken together/ aggregate marks in thequalifying examinations, as prescribed

Note :  Foreign Nationals are not eligible for admission

OTHER STATE BOARDS OF EXAMINATION:

(i)  In respect of candidates qualified from other state board of examinations (of Other States),equivalency certificate has to be obtained from BIE of Telangana State.(ii) Eligible candidate should have obtained 45% (40% marks for reserved categories), in groupsubjects / aggregate in the qualifying examination.

OPEN SCHOOLING (NIOS) / STATE SOCIETY:

(i)   Eligible candidate should have obtained 45% (40% marks for reserved categories), in groupsubjects / aggregate in      the qualifying examination.
 (ii)  Candidates qualified in the said examinations are not eligible for admission into PharmacyCourse. 

INTERMEDIATE VOCATIONAL PASS OUTS :

                                Intermediate Vocational pass outs must pass Mathematics/ Biology and Physical Sciences.

           COPIES OF CERTIFICATES TO BE SUBMITTED: 

(i) 10th class marks memo 
(ii) School Bonafide Certificate
(iii) Intermediate Marks memo & Transfer Certificate 
(iv) Community Certificate 
(v) JEE  Mains Rank Card 
(vi) TSEAMCET  Rank Card 
(vii) Proof of NRI  status
(viii) Equivalency from BIE  for other state Intermediate board/ Universities/ Schools in Othercountries
