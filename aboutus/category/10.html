Prof. Dr. B. Sridhar Reddy completed his Bachelors, Masters & Ph. D in Chemical Engineering and holds a Post graduate Diploma in Industrialization and personal Management. He has around 22 years of rich experience of in Academia, Industry and R&D.
He has published 19 papers in reputed International and National journals to his credit. He is a certified Softskills trainer from Infosys Ltd. He also has affluent experience in Training & Placement. He has been acting as a TV Channel panel expert for many social issues telecasted in various channels. He is also a columnist for engineering education in print media. 

Dr. Reddy  organized and attended many International & National level workshops and seminars. He also visited many Universities and colleges to motivate the students in prime areas of employability skills. He conducted several FDP's mainly on Industry expectations, The Role of a teacher, and Ethics & Values. He received best teacher award for his academic excellence administrative skills & organizational initiatives.

Dr. Reddy   is a Technical committee member in DELLSOFT technologies Pvt Ltd.
